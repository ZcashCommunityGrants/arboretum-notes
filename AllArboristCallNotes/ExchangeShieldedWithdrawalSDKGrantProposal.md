## Executive Summary
This open-source SDK enables cryptocurrency exchanges to integrate Zcash shielded withdrawals with production-ready TypeScript libraries, complete tests, and documentation. Lowering the barrier to adoption for shielded transactions advances the Zcash ecosystem and privacy features.

**Requested Amount:** $38,000 USD (retroactive compensation and ongoing support)

## Key Components
- AddressValidator (ZIP 316 compliant)
- ShieldedTransactionBuilder (ZIP 317 fee support)
- ZcashRpcClient (zcashd/zebrad compatibility)
- SecureKeyManager (AES-256-GCM encryption)
- WithdrawalRateLimiter, AuditLogger, ComplianceManager
- High-level ExchangeShieldedSDK API with DTO boundary methods

## Impact
- Simplifies exchange integration for shielded ZEC withdrawals
- Increases shielded pool size and privacy
- Demonstrates privacy and compliance coexistence
